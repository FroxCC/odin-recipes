Pique Macho

Description

The traditional recipe for pique a lo macho has minced and seasoned meat, sausages, potatoes with sticks, chopped tomato and locoto. Over the years, this recipe has had variations, incorporating onion, sausage, chicken, cheese, egg, and other ingredients.

Steps

  1. Start by peeling the potatoes. After cutting them, it is good to put them in water so that they do not rust. Dry them well before frying them.
  2. Heat a good amount of oil in a frying pan, if you have a thermometer it should reach 160C.
  3. Potatoes are cooked or fried 2 times. The first is to cook the potatoes and the second to give them the crispness that characterizes them.
  4. After the first frying, start cooking the chorizo â€‹â€‹and sausages in a pan with a little oil. Once cooked, reserve on a plate.
  5. Use the same pan to seal and cook the meat. Don't forget to season with salt and pepper.
  6. Once everything is cooked, mix the chorizo, the sausages, the meat; add the fries and mix.
  7. Place this mixture in a large serving dish, and finish the dish by adding the onions, tomatoes, eggs, and chili.
